Y Combinator’s FAQ

Must answer questions before starting any venture!
  1. Potential Users?
  2. Obstacles in your path?
  3. What’s wrong with existing options?
  4. How you’ll overcame the barriers that allow existing options to stay bad?
  5. Who needs what you’re making?
  6. How do you know they need it?
  7. What are they doing now?
  8. What makes you different from existing options?
  9. Why isn’t someone already doing this?
  10. What obstacles will you face and how will you overcame them?
  11. How will customers/users find out about you?
  12. What resistance will they have to trying you?
  13. How will you overcome that resistance?
  14. What are the key things about your field that outsiders don’t understand?
  15. What part of your project are you going to build first?
  16. Who is going to be your first paying customers?
  17. If your startup succeeds, what additional areas might you be able to expand into?
  18. Why did you choose this idea?
  19. What have you learned so far from working on it?
  20. Six months from now, what’s going to be your biggest problem?

Hopefully, I will find an idea that will give satisfying answer for all the above questions and start working on it soon.
