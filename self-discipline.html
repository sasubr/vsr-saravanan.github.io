Self Discipline

When the four corners of this cocoon collide
You’ll slip through the cracks hoping that you’ll survive
Gather your wind, take a deep look inside
Are you really who they idolize?

I'm stuck. I in the SUNKEN PLACE hypnotised by the past. I can think. But I can't do. There is a gap between what I should be doing and what I am doing. I can't seem to do anything. All I need is self discipline. How to bring self discipline in my life and accomplish things I wish to do?

  • Plan your day ahead the night before. PROACTIVE
  • If any new thing arises, promplty make a note of it and that's it. Resist the temptation. Acknowledge the temptation and move ahead.
  • Make mental models about the things I'm going to do. Eg: If you are meeting someone, rehearse what you will be saying and the worst things that can happen so that nothing surprises and you are ready for everything.
  • Make routines and follow them. What to do when you wake up/reach office/reach home/before sleep etc.
  • Meditate daily. COMPULSORY
  • If you break the self discipline, again acknowledge, forgive yourself and start where you left off.
  • Time is my only wealth. Don't waste it. Spend it wisely. Be aware of the shortness of life. Don't waste it.
  • I have 100 things in my mind that I wish to do. It keeps coming back. But right now, just acknowledge the desire. Focus on things that you are supposed/expected to focus. Do things that are expected of you. There is time for other thigs
  • Journal often. Write down your baggage.
