STICK TO THE BASICS

Whenever I find myself in a state of depression, anxiety and restlessness, I need to come back here and read this. Please stick to the basics.

  1. Sleep.
  2. Engage in physical activity.
  3. Write journal and get some perspective.
  4. Eat.
  5. Meet friends.
  6. Clean up my space.

All I need to do is PAUSE AND TAKE A DEEP BREATH physically and mentally.
